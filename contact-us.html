Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free consultations?

Absolutely. Our initial consultation is completely free with zero obligations. During this 15-30 minute call, we'll discuss your business, goals, challenges, and current marketing efforts. We'll ask questions, offer initial insights, and determine if we're a good fit to work together.

There's no sales pitch or pressure. If we're not the right agency for you, we'll be honest about that. If we are, we'll explain the next steps.

How quickly will I hear back from you?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 7 PM IST). If you contact us outside business hours, you'll hear from us by the next business morning.

For urgent matters, call us directly at +91 8708577598 or message on WhatsApp for the fastest response.

Can we meet in person or is it only virtual?

Both! Our office is in Sonipat, Haryana, and we welcome in-person meetings if you're in the area or visiting. We recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the right team members are available.

For clients outside the NCR region, we primarily work virtually through video calls, which has proven highly effective. We serve clients across India and have built successful long-term relationships entirely through remote collaboration.

For major clients or significant projects, we're happy to travel to your location for kickoff meetings or quarterly reviews.

What should I prepare for the first call?

Come prepared to discuss:

  • Your business goals (revenue targets, growth plans, market expansion)
  • Current marketing efforts (what you're doing now, what's working, what isn't)
  • Your target audience (who buys from you and why)
  • Your budget range (even a rough estimate helps us tailor recommendations)
  • Timeline expectations (when do you need to see results?)

Don't worry if you don't have all the answers—that's what the consultation is for. We'll guide the conversation and help you clarify these points.

Do you work with businesses outside India?

While we're based in India and primarily serve Indian businesses, we have experience working with international clients, particularly those targeting Indian markets or having operations in India.

Our expertise is strongest in understanding Indian consumer behavior, regional market nuances, and navigating the Indian digital landscape. If you're an international business looking to enter or grow in the Indian market, we're an excellent fit.

What if I'm not sure what service I need?

That's perfectly normal—and exactly why the consultation exists. Many business owners know they need to improve their digital marketing but aren't sure which services will deliver the best results for their specific situation.

During our call, we'll ask about your business goals, current challenges, and target audience. Based on that conversation, we'll recommend the services that make the most sense for you—whether that's SEO, social media, PPC, a combination, or something else entirely.

Our recommendations are based on what will actually work for your business, not what we want to sell you.

Do you require long-term contracts?

We typically work on 6-12 month agreements, not because we want to lock you in, but because effective digital marketing requires sustained effort to see meaningful results.

SEO takes 4-6 months to show significant results. Brand building doesn't happen overnight. Content marketing compounds over time. Short-term contracts often end right when strategies start delivering results.

That said, we're flexible. If you want to start with a shorter trial period to test our capabilities, we can discuss that. Our goal is to earn your long-term business through results, not contractual obligations.

What makes you different from other agencies?

Honestly? Most agencies will tell you they're "different," "client-focused," and "results-driven." Words are cheap.

Here's what actually makes us different:

  • We say no when appropriate. If your budget isn't realistic for your goals, we'll tell you. If a different approach makes more sense, we'll recommend it—even if it's not our service.
  • Our average client stays 2.5+ years. In an industry where 6-month churn is common, this speaks to our results and relationships.
  • Transparent reporting. You see exactly what we do and what results we achieve. No vanity metrics or confusing dashboards.
  • Direct access. You work with actual strategists, not account coordinators reading from scripts.

But don't take our word for it—talk to our clients or review our case studies. Results speak louder than marketing claims.
