1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out contact forms on our website
  • Request a consultation or quote
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Engage our services and become a client
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or social media
  • Participate in surveys or provide feedback

This personal information may include:

  • Name (first and last name)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name and position
  • Business address
  • Website URL
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Any other information you choose to provide in communications

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time and date of visits
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device identifiers
  • Geographic location data

Information from Clients

When you engage our services, we may collect additional information necessary to deliver our marketing services:

  • Access to your website analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media account access and data
  • Advertising platform credentials and data
  • Business performance metrics
  • Customer data (when necessary for marketing campaigns)
  • Content, images, and brand materials

For Marketing and Analytics

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers and Business Partners

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business and delivering services, including:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Email marketing platforms
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Payment processors
  • CRM and project management tools
  • Advertising platforms (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues
  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and any choices you may have.

With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (Google Analytics, etc.). These cookies collect aggregated, anonymous data.

Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites and display relevant advertisements. These may be set by our advertising partners.

Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices to enhance your experience.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close your browser

Third-Party Tracking

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data about your use of our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Security Measures Include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Encrypted backups of client data
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Role in Security

You also play a role in protecting your information:

  • Keep your login credentials confidential
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Log out of accounts when using shared devices
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep your contact information up to date

6. Your Rights and Choices

Under Indian data protection laws and in accordance with our commitment to transparency, you have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will provide you with a copy of your data in a commonly used electronic format.

Right to Correction

If you believe any information we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request that we correct or update it.

Right to Deletion

You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions (such as information we must retain for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes).

Right to Object

You can object to our processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes at any time.

Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used format and transmit it to another service provider.

Marketing Communications

You can opt out of receiving marketing emails by:

  • Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email
  • Contacting us directly at nitin@goddigitalmarketing.com
  • Updating your communication preferences in your account settings

Note: Even if you opt out of marketing communications, we may still send you service-related communications.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at nitin@goddigitalmarketing.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website and services may contain links to third-party websites, applications, or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to these third-party services.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Google Services: Google Analytics, Google Ads, Google Tag Manager
  • Social Media Platforms: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram
  • Email Marketing: Email service providers for newsletter distribution
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment gateways for billing
  • Hosting Providers: Website and data hosting services

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external services.

Social Media Features

Our website may include social media features such as share buttons. These features may collect your IP address, track which pages you visit, and may set a cookie. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing them.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention Periods:

  • Contact Form Inquiries: Retained for 2 years or until you request deletion
  • Client Information: Retained for the duration of our business relationship plus 7 years for legal and tax compliance
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Website Analytics: Retained according to Google Analytics policies (typically 26 months)
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law

When we no longer need your information, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention and deletion policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at nitin@goddigitalmarketing.com. We will take steps to delete such information from our systems.

10. International Data Transfers

While we are based in India and primarily serve Indian clients, some of our service providers may be located in other countries. When we transfer your personal information internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

These safeguards may include:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Ensuring the receiving country has adequate data protection laws
  • Obtaining your explicit consent for the transfer

If you have questions about international data transfers, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

When we make material changes to this policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you via email if you are a current client or subscriber
  • Display a prominent notice on our website
  • Request your consent if required by law

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Your continued use of our website and services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
