Amritsar PPC: Marketing Sikhism's Holiest City

Amritsar stands as Sikhism's most sacred city, home to the Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib) that draws millions of pilgrims and tourists annually from across India and the globe. This religious tourism dominance creates an economy centered on hospitality, food services, religious tourism operators, and traditional Punjabi crafts and textiles. Beyond tourism, Amritsar's textile industry produces traditional Punjabi suits, phulkari embroidery, and handicrafts. The city's border location near Wagah creates unique cross-border commerce dynamics. This blend of religious tourism appeal with traditional commerce creates distinct opportunities for PPC advertising in Amritsar. From heritage hotels near Golden Temple to textile exporters targeting international Punjabi diaspora, from tour operators organizing Golden Temple visits to restaurants serving traditional langar-style cuisine commercially, Amritsar's economy demands digital advertising strategies that respect religious sentiments while reaching diverse tourist and commercial audiences.
