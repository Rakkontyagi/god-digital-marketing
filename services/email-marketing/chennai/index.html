Understanding email performance in Chennai requires recognizing the city's economic foundations—automotive and manufacturing dominance combined with growing IT services strength and significant healthcare and education sectors. Each sector requires distinct email approaches while sharing Chennai's common culture of quality focus and methodical evaluation.

IT Services & Technology Email Marketing: Chennai's IT sector differs from Bangalore's startup energy—it leans toward stable, process-driven service delivery for global clients.
