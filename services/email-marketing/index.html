Email Isn't Dead—Bad Email Is Dead

I hear it all the time: "Email doesn't work anymore. Nobody opens emails. It's all about social media now."

Wrong. Dead wrong.

Email marketing generates an average ROI of ₹36 for every ₹1 spent. That's 3600% ROI. Show me another marketing channel that comes close to those numbers. Social media? Paid ads? Content marketing? All valuable, but none deliver ROI like email.

The problem isn't email. The problem is boring, irrelevant, poorly-timed emails that treat subscribers like ATM machines instead of people.

Think about your own inbox. You ignore 90% of marketing emails, right? But there are probably 5-10 brands whose emails you actually look forward to receiving. Maybe it's a newsletter that teaches you something valuable. Or a brand that always has great offers timed perfectly. Or someone whose writing style makes you smile.

That's the email marketing we create. Not spam. Not generic blasts. Strategic, personalized, valuable emails that people actually want to receive.

Here's our philosophy: Your email list is a privilege, not a right. Every subscriber trusted you with their inbox—the most personal digital space they have. We honor that trust by sending emails that add value, respect their time, and only promote when it's genuinely relevant.

The Email Types That Actually Make Money

Not all emails serve the same purpose. Here are the campaigns that consistently deliver results:

Welcome Series

Purpose: Make a great first impression and set expectations with new subscribers.

Why it works: Welcome emails have 4x higher open rates and 5x higher click rates than regular campaigns. New subscribers are your hottest leads—strike while the iron is hot.

What we include: Thank you message, brand story, expectation setting, valuable content or offer, social proof, clear next steps.

Regular Newsletters

Purpose: Stay top-of-mind, provide value, and maintain the relationship.

Why it works: Consistency builds trust. Subscribers who regularly engage with your newsletter are 3x more likely to purchase when the timing is right.

What we include: Curated valuable content, industry insights, company updates, soft product mentions, clear call-to-action.

Abandoned Cart Emails

Purpose: Recover lost sales from customers who added products but didn't complete checkout.

Why it works: Average cart abandonment rate is 70%. Sending 3 well-timed emails can recover 15-20% of those carts. That's found money.

What we include: Product reminder, urgency elements, social proof, incentive (if needed), easy checkout link, customer support offer.

Promotional Campaigns

Purpose: Drive immediate sales during special offers, launches, or seasonal promotions.

Why it works: When done strategically (not constantly), promotional emails convert 3-5x higher than regular newsletters.

What we include: Compelling offer, clear value proposition, urgency/scarcity, stunning visuals, prominent CTA, mobile optimization.

Re-engagement Campaigns

Purpose: Win back inactive subscribers before they're gone forever.

Why it works: Re-engaging existing subscribers is 10x cheaper than acquiring new ones. Even a 5% win-back rate significantly impacts your bottom line.

What we include: "We miss you" messaging, special win-back offer, preference center option, honest conversation, easy unsubscribe (better than being ignored).

Post-Purchase Sequences

Purpose: Confirm purchase, provide support, encourage reviews, and set up the next purchase.

Why it works: Acquiring a customer is hard. Getting them to buy again is much easier. Post-purchase emails boost repeat purchase rates by 25-40%.

What we include: Order confirmation, shipping updates, usage tips, review request, cross-sell/upsell offers, loyalty program invitation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do we grow our email list?

We use multiple strategies: valuable lead magnets (ebooks, guides, checklists), content upgrades on blog posts, exit-intent popups, social media campaigns, and optimized website signup forms. The key is offering something valuable in exchange for the email address—not just "sign up for our newsletter."

How often should we send emails?

It depends on your business and audience. E-commerce brands often send 3-5x weekly (promotional + newsletter). B2B companies typically send 1-2x weekly. The key is consistency and value—if every email provides value, subscribers will welcome them. If you only email when you want to sell something, they'll tune out.

What's a good email open rate?

Industry averages vary, but generally: 15-25% is average, 25-35% is good, 35%+ is excellent. However, open rates are becoming less reliable due to Apple's Mail Privacy Protection. We focus on click rates and conversions—metrics that directly impact revenue.

Which email marketing platform do you recommend?

We're platform-agnostic and work with all major ESPs: Mailchimp, Klaviyo, HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, and more. The best platform depends on your business size, budget, and needs. We'll recommend the right fit and handle migration if you're switching platforms.

Can you help with email deliverability issues?

Absolutely. If your emails are landing in spam or you're seeing low delivery rates, we conduct a deliverability audit. We check sender reputation, authentication records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), list quality, content issues, and engagement rates—then fix what's broken.

How do you measure email marketing ROI?

We track revenue directly attributed to email campaigns using conversion tracking and UTM parameters. We calculate total revenue generated from email, subtract email marketing costs (platform fees + our management fee), and show you the exact ROI. Most clients see 20-40x ROI from strategic email marketing.

Do you create the email designs?

Yes! Our team includes email designers who create custom, mobile-responsive templates that match your brand. We handle everything from design to HTML coding to testing across email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.).
