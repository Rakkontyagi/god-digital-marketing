The Metrics That Actually Matter

Not all metrics are created equal. Here's what we focus on—and why:

Revenue & ROI

What it tells you: How much money your marketing actually generates and whether it's profitable.

Why it matters: Traffic doesn't pay bills. Revenue does. Every marketing channel should demonstrate positive ROI or be eliminated.

What we track: Revenue by channel, ROI by campaign, customer acquisition cost (CAC), customer lifetime value (CLV), payback period.

Conversion Rates

What it tells you: How effectively you're turning visitors into leads or customers.

Why it matters: A 1% improvement in conversion rate can double revenue without spending more on traffic.

What we track: Overall conversion rate, conversion rate by traffic source, landing page conversion rates, micro-conversions, funnel drop-off points.

User Behavior Patterns

What it tells you: How visitors actually use your website—not how you think they use it.

Why it matters: Understanding behavior reveals friction points and optimization opportunities worth thousands in revenue.

What we track: User flows, pages per session, time on key pages, scroll depth, click patterns, form field interactions.

Customer Lifetime Value

What it tells you: How much revenue a customer generates over their entire relationship with your business.

Why it matters: Knowing CLV determines how much you can afford to spend acquiring customers and stay profitable.

What we track: Average CLV, CLV by acquisition channel, CLV by customer segment, repeat purchase rate, churn rate.

Channel Performance

What it tells you: Which marketing channels deliver the best results and deserve more investment.

Why it matters: Most businesses waste 40-60% of their marketing budget on underperforming channels.

What we track: Traffic by channel, conversions by channel, cost per acquisition by channel, assisted conversions, attribution modeling.

Real Results From Data-Driven Decisions

E-commerce Brand, Mumbai

Challenge: An online furniture store was spending ₹4.5 lakhs monthly on marketing but couldn't determine which channels were actually profitable. They suspected waste but didn't have data to prove it.

What We Did:

  • Implemented comprehensive e-commerce tracking
  • Set up multi-touch attribution modeling
  • Analyzed customer lifetime value by acquisition channel
  • Tracked full customer journey from first visit to purchase
  • Identified high-value customer segments
  • Created automated monthly reporting dashboard

Discoveries:

  • Display ads drove traffic but had 68% higher CAC than other channels
  • Instagram ads delivered 3.2x higher CLV than Facebook ads
  • Email marketing had 12.8x ROI but was only getting 8% of budget
  • Organic search customers had 42% higher repeat purchase rates
  • Mobile users converted at half the rate of desktop (UX issue)

Actions Taken & Results:

  • Cut display ad budget by 75% → Saved ₹1.8 lakhs monthly
  • Shifted budget to Instagram and email → 2.4x increase in ROAS
  • Fixed mobile checkout flow → 58% improvement in mobile conversions
  • Created customer segments → Personalized campaigns increased CLV by 35%
  • Overall result: Same marketing budget, ₹28 lakhs more monthly revenue

B2B SaaS Company, Bangalore

Challenge: A project management SaaS had solid trial signup numbers but terrible trial-to-paid conversion (only 8%). They didn't know why users weren't converting.

What We Did:

Frequently Asked Questions

What analytics tools do you use?

We primarily use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) as the foundation, supplemented by Google Tag Manager, heatmap tools (Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity), Search Console, and platform-specific analytics (Facebook Ads, Google Ads, etc.). We'll work with whatever tools you already have or recommend the best stack for your needs.

How long before we see insights?

Basic insights are available immediately if you already have analytics running. For comprehensive analysis, we need at least 30 days of clean data. Deep insights (customer journey analysis, attribution modeling) require 60-90 days to establish patterns and statistical significance.

Can you fix our existing analytics setup?

Absolutely. We conduct comprehensive analytics audits to identify tracking gaps, configuration errors, and data quality issues—then fix everything to ensure accurate, reliable data going forward. Most setups have critical tracking errors that invalidate data.

How often will we receive reports?

Standard reporting is monthly, with comprehensive analysis and strategic recommendations. We can also provide weekly reports for campaigns that need closer monitoring or quarterly reports for high-level strategic planning. You'll have 24/7 access to live dashboards.

What's the difference between reporting and analysis?

Reporting tells you what happened (traffic increased 15%). Analysis tells you why it happened and what to do about it (traffic increased because of blog post X ranking #1 for keyword Y—here are 10 similar content opportunities). We provide both, with emphasis on actionable analysis.

Can you track ROI for offline conversions?

Yes! We can implement offline conversion tracking by connecting your CRM, importing sale data, using call tracking, implementing unique promo codes, and building attribution models that connect online behavior to offline purchases. It requires some technical setup but is definitely possible.

Is our data secure and private?

Absolutely. We sign NDAs, use secure access protocols, never share client data, and follow all privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.). Your data stays in your accounts—we just access it for analysis and reporting. We can also work within your existing security frameworks.
