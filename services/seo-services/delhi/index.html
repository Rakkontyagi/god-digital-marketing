Delhi NCR isn't one market—it's a collection of distinct markets with different search behaviors, demographics, and commercial intent. What works for our SEO clients in Bangalore won't necessarily work here.

Key Delhi SEO considerations:

  • Multi-city complexity: Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Ghaziabad, Faridabad each have distinct search patterns
  • Extreme competition: Virtually every industry is saturated with competitors investing in paid advertising and organic search
  • High commercial intent: Delhi searchers know what they want and compare extensively before buying
  • Mobile dominance: 75%+ of searches happen on mobile, demanding mobile-first optimization
  • Multilingual needs: Hindi, English, and Punjabi search optimization often required
  • Hyperlocal focus: "Near me" searches and neighborhood-specific queries dominate

Our Delhi-Specific SEO Methodology

Local Search Dominance

For Delhi businesses, local SEO is non-negotiable. Our local search optimization approach includes:

  • Google Business Profile optimization: Complete setup for Delhi location with category optimization, posts, photos, and reviews
  • Multi-location strategies: Separate optimization for each NCR location (Delhi, Gurugram, Noida)
  • Citation building: Listings in Delhi-specific directories, industry associations, and local business platforms
  • Review management: Systematic generation and response to Google reviews
  • Local link building: Partnerships with Delhi businesses, event sponsorships, local media coverage

Neighborhood-Level Content Strategy

Generic "Delhi" content doesn't rank. Specific does. We create hyperlocal content that targets:

  • Service pages for each neighborhood (e.g., "Dermatologist in Lajpat Nagar")
  • Location-specific landing pages optimized for conversion
  • Area guides that establish local authority
  • Neighborhood-focused blog posts addressing local concerns
  • Customer stories and case studies from specific Delhi areas

Technical SEO Foundation

With 75%+ mobile traffic, technical performance determines success. Our technical SEO audit and optimization covers:

  • Mobile speed optimization: Target load time under 2 seconds on 4G
  • Core Web Vitals: Optimization for LCP, FID, CLS scores
  • Schema markup: Local business, product, FAQ, and review schema
  • Site architecture: Clean URL structure, internal linking, crawlability
  • Security: HTTPS, security headers, data protection compliance

Competitive Intelligence
