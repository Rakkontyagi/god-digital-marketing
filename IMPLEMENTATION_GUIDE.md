## üé® CONTENT CREATION BEST PRACTICES

### Writing Quality Standards:

**1. Sentence Variety (CRITICAL for AI Detection Avoidance):**
- Short (5-8 words): "Results matter. Rankings don't pay bills."
- Medium (12-20 words): "We've helped over 150 Mumbai businesses grow through strategic, data-driven SEO that delivers measurable results."
- Long (25-35 words): "When someone in Bandra searches for Italian restaurants near them, they're not looking for options across Mumbai‚Äîthey want recommendations within walking distance, with current menus, and recent reviews from people they trust."
- **Mix ratios:** 20% short, 50% medium, 30% long

**2. Paragraph Structure:**
- Vary between 1-5 sentences
- Never more than 3 paragraphs in a row of same length
- Use one-sentence paragraphs for emphasis: "That's why SEO matters."

**3. Transition Phrases (Use Different Ones):**
- Addition: Moreover, Additionally, Beyond that, What's more, On top of this
- Contrast: However, Yet, That said, Conversely, On the other hand
- Example: For instance, Consider, Take, Think about, Look at
- Result: Therefore, As a result, This means, Consequently
- Emphasis: Indeed, In fact, Notably, Crucially, Importantly

**4. Personal Touch Elements:**
- "In our experience..."
- "We've found that..."
- "Here's what we've learned:"
- "The reality is..."
- "Let's be honest‚Äî"
- "Here's the truth:"

**5. Specific Over Generic:**
- ‚ùå "We provide excellent service"
- ‚úÖ "Our average client sees 3.2x traffic increase within 6 months"

- ‚ùå "We have experienced team"
- ‚úÖ "Our SEO specialists have 8+ years average experience and manage ‚Çπ5+ crore in client SEO budgets annually"

**6. Industry-Specific Language:**
Each industry has unique terminology. Use it naturally:
- Healthcare: Patients, appointments, specialists, E-A-T, YMYL
- Real Estate: Properties, listings, brokers, neighborhoods, RERA
- E-commerce: Conversions, cart abandonment, product feeds, SKUs
- Legal: Practice areas, cases, clients, bar association
